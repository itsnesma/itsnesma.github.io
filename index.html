About

01 / About

I'm a multidisciplinary maker who got into the web because it was the closest thing to magic I could find. Years later, I'm still chasing that same feeling — only now I get to share it with other people.

My work spans interactive design, frontend engineering, and the occasional research detour. I care about details that nobody asked for, typography that earns its keep, and interfaces that don't take themselves too seriously.

When I'm not at a keyboard, you'll find me sketching, reading dense books I don't fully understand, and pretending I'll learn to play the synth this year.
